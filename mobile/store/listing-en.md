## Description (both stores)
Stack is a private accountability app for small groups of 2–8 people who push
each other to show up — every single day.

Here's how it works:
• Every day you work out, you take a check-in photo right in the app.
• Your crew sees it instantly in a shared feed.
• Your streak grows. Miss a day, and everyone knows.

No followers. No likes from strangers. No endless scrolling. Just you and the
handful of people whose opinion actually keeps you honest.

FEATURES
• Daily photo check-ins — proof you showed up
• Private groups with simple invite codes
• Live group feed with real-time updates
• Personal streaks that reward consistency, not intensity
• Push notifications when your crew shows up — and when your streak is at risk
• Works alongside the Stack web app; one account everywhere

Stack is built for the small crew that wants to get in shape together and stay
accountable. Create a group, invite your people, and don't be the one who breaks
the streak.
