# Screenshots — required sizes & shot list

Screenshots can't be auto-generated; capture them from the running app on a
device or simulator. Tip: `npx expo start`, open on a simulator, navigate to
each screen, and use the simulator's screenshot tool.

## Apple App Store (required)
At least one set is required; the 6.7" set is reused for smaller devices.

| Device | Resolution (portrait) | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 6.7" iPhone (15/16 Pro Max) | 1290 × 2796 | **Required** |
| 6.5" iPhone (optional) | 1242 × 2688 | Optional |
| 13" iPad (only if you ship iPad) | 2064 × 2752 | `supportsTablet` is true |

Min 3, max 10 per device.

## Google Play (required)
| Asset | Size |
| --- | --- |
| Phone screenshots (2–8) | min 1080 px on the short side, 16:9 or 9:16 |
| Feature graphic | 1024 × 500 (**required**) |
| App icon | 512 × 512 (from `assets/icon.png`) |

## Suggested shot list (5 screens)
1. **Home feed** — the group's check-ins with a live streak badge. Caption:
   "Your crew. One photo a day."
2. **Check-in** — the camera/photo step. Caption: "Proof you showed up."
3. **Streak on profile** — a big number. Caption: "Don't break the streak."
4. **Groups** — a crew with an invite code. Caption: "Small, private, yours."
5. **Push notification** — a banner mockup. Caption: "Everyone knows when you
   miss."

Keep the dark `#0A0A0B` background and the volt `#C6F806` accent consistent with
the brand.
